Empowering Rwanda Through Responsible Finance

Pearl Capital Ltd is a micro-lending institution dedicated to fueling economic growth by providing tailored financial solutions to SMEs, salaried individuals, and farmers across Rwanda.

Our Mission

Our Mission & Vision

At Pearl Capital, our mission is to provide accessible, transparent, and responsible financial services that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their economic potential. We believe that financial inclusion is key to sustainable development.

Our vision is to be Rwanda's leading micro-lending partner, recognized for our commitment to client success, ethical practices, and positive social impact.

Client-Centric

We prioritize our clients' needs and success above all else, offering personalized service and flexible solutions.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations, ensuring transparency and trust.

Innovation

We continuously develop new financial products and services that meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Collaboration

We work closely with clients, partners, and communities to create shared value and sustainable impact.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted financial partner for thousands of Rwandans.

2023

Foundation

Pearl Capital Ltd was established by a group of financial experts with a shared vision of addressing the credit gap for SMEs and individuals in Rwanda.

2024

First 1,000 Clients

Within our first year of operation, we successfully served over 1,000 clients, disbursing loans that helped businesses expand and families achieve financial stability.

2025

Expansion & Recognition

We expanded our services to agricultural finance and received recognition for our responsible lending practices from the Rwanda Development Board.

Present

Ongoing Commitment

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, remaining committed to our mission of empowering Rwandans through accessible finance.

Governance & Compliance

We adhere to the highest standards of corporate governance and regulatory compliance.

Regulatory Compliance

Pearl Capital Ltd operates in full compliance with Rwandan financial regulations and international best practices in microfinance.

  • Registered with the Rwanda Development Board
  • Compliant with National Bank of Rwanda guidelines
  • Regular external audits and reporting
  • Transparent pricing and fee disclosure

Risk Management

We maintain robust risk management frameworks to protect our clients and ensure the sustainability of our operations.

  • Comprehensive credit assessment processes
  • Diversified loan portfolio
  • Regular monitoring and evaluation
  • Client education on financial management
